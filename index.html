Our Mission

Connecting People to the Fullness of Life in Christ

We exist to connect people to the fullness of life found in Jesus Christ through the ancient faith of the Orthodox Church.

Come As You Are

People who were nothing like Jesus gravitated toward Him, and what was true of Christ should be true of us. Regardless of your background, your past, or where you are in your spiritual journey, you are welcome here. Our Savior continually invites us to come and see.

Transcendent Worship

Liturgical and personal worship point us to something much greater than ourselves. Through prayer, Scripture, the Divine Liturgy, and the sacramental life of the Church, we encounter Christ and are transformed by His grace. The worship of the Orthodox Church is rooted in the faith handed down from the Apostles and preserved through the centuries.

Become the Light

Christ calls His people to carry His light into the world. For nearly 2,000 years, Christians have gathered around the Divine Liturgy and then gone forth to share Christ's love with their communities. We seek to continue that same mission today by bringing the beauty, truth, and fullness of the Orthodox faith to Des Moines and beyond.

Come and See

For 2,000 years Christians have gathered around the same faith, worship, and sacraments handed down by the Apostles. We invite you to experience the beauty of Orthodox Christianity and discover the fullness of life found in Jesus Christ.
