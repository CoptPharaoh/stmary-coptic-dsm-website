Welcome to St. Mary

You Are Welcome Here

Welcome to St. Mary Coptic Orthodox Church. You do not need to be Egyptian, Coptic, or Orthodox to visit. Whether you are exploring Christianity, returning to church, visiting with a friend, or simply curious about ancient Christian worship, you are welcome to come, observe, pray, ask questions, and learn.

Our parish is a family gathered around Christ in prayer, worship, fellowship, and the sacramental life of the Church. We would be honored to meet you and help you feel at home.

Come, observe, pray, and ask questions. There is a place for you here.

What to Expect at a Coptic Orthodox Service

Ancient Worship, Reverence, and Prayer

Coptic Orthodox worship is ancient, biblical, sacramental, and deeply reverent. Visitors are welcome to observe the service, follow along as much as they can, and speak with Abouna or a servant afterward.

A

Ancient Christian Worship

The Divine Liturgy carries the faith and prayer of the early Church.

C

Chanting

Services include chanting in English, Coptic, and Arabic.

I

Incense

Incense expresses prayer rising before God and fills the church with reverence.

S

Standing and Prayer

Orthodox worship often includes standing, bowing, and repeated responses.

G

Scripture Readings

The Epistles, Acts, Psalms, Gospel, and prayers are woven throughout the service.

L

The Divine Liturgy

The Liturgy gathers the Church around Christ and the Eucharist.

E

Reverence Around the Eucharist

Visitors are welcome to observe, while Holy Communion is received by prepared Orthodox Christians.

Q

Questions Are Welcome

After the service, you are welcome to speak with Abouna or a servant.
