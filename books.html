Arabic PDF books can be added under assets/books/arabic/pope-shenouda/. This section is ready for Arabic book cards as soon as the PDFs are placed in the folder.

Ready for Arabic PDFs

Add Arabic book PDFs to assets/books/arabic/pope-shenouda/, then copy the commented card template below and update the title, description, and filename.

Adding Books

Folder Structure

Place English PDFs in assets/books/english/pope-shenouda/ and Arabic PDFs in assets/books/arabic/pope-shenouda/. Additional authors or categories can use the same pattern inside each language folder.
